NPM Thrift SHOP

About Us

Our Vision

At NPM Thrift Shop, we envision a world where sustainable shopping is the norm. We believe in reducing waste while providing our community with unique, affordable fashion choices. Our goal is to inspire conscious consumerism and promote a circular economy.

Our Mission

Our mission is to offer a curated selection of high-quality second-hand items, empowering our customers to make eco-friendly choices without sacrificing style. We strive to create a welcoming space that fosters creativity and individuality, supporting both our shoppers and local charities.

Brief History

Founded in 2010, NPM Thrift Shop began as a small community initiative aimed at providing gently used clothing and goods to those in need. Over the years, we have grown into a beloved local treasure, known for our eclectic inventory and commitment to sustainability. Our journey is fueled by a passion for fashion and a dedication to giving back.
